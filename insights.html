Care Insights

Acne

Acne usually happens when pores get blocked with oil and dead skin. Hormones, stress, or harsh products can make it worse. A calm, balanced routine helps reduce breakouts over time.

Pigmentation

Dark spots and uneven tone often appear after sun exposure or acne. Gentle care and daily protection help skin regain clarity slowly.

Sensitive Skin

If your skin reacts easily, it usually means the barrier is weak. Simple routines and fewer products help restore comfort.

Dandruff

Flakes and itching often come from scalp imbalance. The right cleansing routine can bring relief and control.

Hair Fall

Hair fall may increase due to stress, nutrition, or scalp health. Understanding the cause is the first step to managing it.

Oily Scalp

An oily scalp can make hair greasy quickly and clog follicles. Balanced washing keeps the scalp fresh and healthy.
