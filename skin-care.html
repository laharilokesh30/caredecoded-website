Skin Care

Skin care doesn’t have to be confusing. This guide helps you understand your skin, common problems, ingredients that matter, and how to build a safe routine.

Know Your Skin Type

Normal Skin

Balanced oil and moisture. Few breakouts, comfortable feel.

Dry Skin

Feels tight or rough. Needs hydration and barrier repair.

Oily Skin

Excess oil, shiny appearance, acne-prone.

Combination Skin

Oily in some areas, dry in others (usually T-zone).

Sensitive Skin

Reacts easily to products, weather, or ingredients.

Common Skin Problems

  • Acne & pimples
  • Dark spots & pigmentation
  • Dryness & flaking
  • Excess oil & clogged pores
  • Sensitivity & irritation

Ingredients That Matter

Niacinamide

Helps with oil control, pores, and barrier strength.

Salicylic Acid

Unclogs pores and helps acne-prone skin.

Hyaluronic Acid

Deep hydration for all skin types.

Ceramides

Repair the skin barrier and prevent moisture loss.

Basic Safe Skin Care Routine

  1. Gentle Cleanser
  2. Treatment / Serum (if needed)
  3. Moisturizer
  4. Sunscreen (morning only)

Consistency matters more than expensive products.
